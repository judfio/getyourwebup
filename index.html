About Me

Jude

I am a native New Yorker, now living in New Jersey with my husband and three children. My experience in tech began at NYU, where I studied for my BA in Computer Science. After graduation, I worked for JPMorgan and United Parcel Service over a 12 year period. I was involved in every aspect of the development lifecycle, from planning to development to testing.

After taking some time off to be home with my children, I reentered the workforce to become an Assistive Technology Specialist in a school district in Northern NJ. I love my work but I've been longing for a return to my programming roots. I decided to take a course to learn about Front-End Web Development and the site you are looking at was hand crafted for class.

While the application may be new, the essential skills of development remain the same as they were in my early career. My emphasis continues to be creating applications that work well, adhere to best practices, are easy to maintain, and provide complete satisfaction to the client.

Guiding Principles

Communication

Understanding client needs and maintaining open communication throughout project development is essential to success.

Quality

Always start with a design plan! Then, create clean, tidy, and efficient code that matches the client's exact specifications.

Testing

Planned, rigorous testing ensures consistent & reliable performance across browsers and platforms.

Documentation

It's not just about the code! Good documentation makes a project transferable and easier to maintain.
